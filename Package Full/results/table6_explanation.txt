
-----------------------------------------------------------------
DECOMPOSITION OF SUPEROCC EVOLUTION - EXPLANATION VARIABLES
-----------------------------------------------------------------

 begin_distr: the initial distribution (1984)
 end_distr_d: the end distribution in the data(2012)
 end_distr_m: end distribution in the model
 decomp_exofl_m: the change in size through retirement/entry in diff. occ
 decomp_endofl_m: the change in occsize through net mobility of the unempl.
 decomp_endo_gd_m: the part of endofl change occuring when urate<median
 decomp_endo_bad_m: the part of endofl change occuring when urate>=median
